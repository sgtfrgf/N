About PM Modi

Narendra Damodardas Modi was born on 17 September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat. He served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and has been the Prime Minister of India since 2014.

Known for his leadership style and economic policies, Modi has implemented various initiatives aimed at modernizing India's infrastructure, improving governance, and enhancing India's global standing.

Before becoming Prime Minister, Modi was a member of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and later joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). His political journey has been marked by a strong focus on development, technology, and national security.

Key Achievements

Economic Reforms

Implemented GST, demonetization, and various economic policies to strengthen the Indian economy.

Ayushman Bharat

Launched the world's largest healthcare program to provide medical coverage to millions of Indians.

Swachh Bharat

Initiated the Clean India Mission to improve sanitation and waste management across the country.

Renewable Energy

Expanded India's renewable energy capacity and committed to climate change initiatives.
